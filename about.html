About the Penwoman

She writes with a scalpel, not a pen. Her stories don’t heal—they haunt. Ava Valentine, known to the wrecked world as PAV, doesn’t tell tales. She bleeds truth, stitched in fiction, whispered through chaos.

Based in Paris. Fueled by heartbreak, BLACKPINK, and the soft tremble of undone love. She writes women who feel too much, love too hard, and fall too deep.

Every chapter is a confession. Every book, a war cry. This is not a safe space. This is a beautiful, burning wreck.
